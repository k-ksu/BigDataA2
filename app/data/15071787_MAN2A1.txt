Alpha-mannosidase 2 is an enzyme that in humans is encoded by the MAN2A1 gene. == Function == This gene encodes a protein which is a member of family 38 of the glycosyl hydrolases. The protein is located in the Golgi apparatus and catalyzes the final hydrolytic step in the asparagine-linked oligosaccharide (N-linked glycosylation) maturation pathway.
