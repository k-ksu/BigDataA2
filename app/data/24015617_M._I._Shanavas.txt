M. I. Shanavas (22 September 1951 – 21 November 2018) was an Indian politician. He was the working president of the Kerala Pradesh Congress Committee and a Member of Parliament of the 16th Lok Sabha of India. ==Biography== M. I. Shanavas represented the Wayanad constituency and was a member of the Indian National Congress. He was a member of the Kerala Pradesh Congress Committee since 1983.
