Mahsa University is a private medical university in Petaling Jaya, Malaysia. It was founded in Pusat Bandar Damansara in 2005 as a college, and was upgraded to university status in 2009. The university offers academic programs in various fields including medicine, dentistry, nursing, pharmacy, engineering, business, and law.
