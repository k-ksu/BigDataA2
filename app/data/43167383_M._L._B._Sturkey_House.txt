M. L. B. Sturkey House is a historic home located at McCormick in McCormick County, South Carolina. It was built about 1895, and is a one-story, frame cottage with Queen Anne style detailing. It consists of gable front main section, with a wing and a rear addition. It was built by M. L. B. Sturkey, one of McCormick's most prominent early residents and leader in the formation of McCormick County.
