M.S.N. Charities is a charitable organisation established at East Godavari District of Andhra Pradesh, India, in 1915, from the will of Malladi Satyalingam Naicker, who died on 29 January 1915. ==Malladi Satyalingam Naicker == thumb|The Bronze statue of founder of MSN Charities Sri Malladi Satyalingam Naicker Malladi Satyalingam Naicker belongs to the Agnikulakshatriya Community. He was born in Coringa, a small village near Kakinada about 1843. Satyalingam Naicker died in Rangoon on 29 January 1915 when he was about 75 years old. ==References== Category:Indian humanitarians Category:Charities based in India Category:Organisations based in Andhra Pradesh Category:1915 establishments in India Category:Organizations established in 1915
