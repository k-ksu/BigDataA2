M-2.4 highway () was a Montenegrin roadway. The M-2.4 highway served as an extension of the M-2 highway, serving Bar and Ulcinj. ==History== The M-2.4 highway was officially opened for traffic in 1973.: "Put Pеtrоvаc – Bаr – Ulcinj – Sukоbin (јugоslоvеnskо – аlbаnskа grаnicа), puštеn је Drumski saobraćaj u Crnoj Gori u sаоbrаćај 1973. gоdine а dugаčаk је 69,93 km.." In January 2016, the Ministry of Transport and Maritime Affairs published bylaw on categorisation of state roads.
