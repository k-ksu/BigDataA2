M. Tamilkudimagan was an Indian politician and Member of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly. He served as Speaker of the Assembly from 1989 to 1991. His original name was Sathiah. He was the DMK Minister for Tamil Language and Culture in the 1996 Cabinet.
