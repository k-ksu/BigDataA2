M.R. High School and Jr College is one of the old schools and Jr colleges in Gadhinglaj, Kolhapur district, State Maharashtra, India. This school is on Kadgaon (Village near Gadhinglaj) Road. This school is run by "District Council or Zila Parishad or Zilla Parishad, District Panchayat, or Zila Panchayat". The high school has education available from 1st standard to 10th standard. It also has a Junior college stream which has 11th and 12th standards of Science Stream. The school has a playground inside the campus.
