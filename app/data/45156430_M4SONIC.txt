Nick Boundy, better known for his stage name M4SONIC ( ), is an Australian electronic music producer, DJ and controllerist who was previously signed to Sony / Ultra Music. He co-produced (with Stargate) the Ylvis comedy song "The Fox (What Does the Fox Say?)".
