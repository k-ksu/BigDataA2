M. P. Nachimuthu Mudaliyar (28 March 1913 – 27 June 1987) was a Padma Shri award winner in 1983 for his social work in the hand loom field. He was the founder of Chentex Hand Looms in Chennimalai. An Engineering College is constructed in Chennimalai with his name by J.
