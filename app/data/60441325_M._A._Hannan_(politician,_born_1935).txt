M. A. Hannan (, 11 April 1935 – 15 June 2021) was a Bangladeshi politician who served as Member of Parliament from Mymensingh-7. ==Early life== Hannan was born on 11 April 1935. He completed his undergraduate from the University of Dhaka. ==Career== Hannan was elected to Parliament from Mymensingh-7 as a Jatiya Party candidate in 2014 and served till 2018.
