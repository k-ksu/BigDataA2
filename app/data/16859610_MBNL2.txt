Muscleblind-like protein 2 is a protein that in humans is encoded by the MBNL2 gene. This gene encodes a C3H-type zinc finger protein, which is similar to the Drosophila melanogaster muscleblind B protein. Drosophila muscleblind is a gene required for photoreceptor differentiation.
