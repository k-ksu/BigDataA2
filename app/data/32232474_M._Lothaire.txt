M. Lothaire is the pseudonym of a group of mathematicians, many of whom were students of Marcel-Paul Schützenberger. The name is used as the author of several of their joint books about combinatorics on words. The group is named for Lothair I.. == Members == Mathematicians in the group have included Jean- Paul Allouche, Jean Berstel,..
