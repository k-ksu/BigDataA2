Municipal Basketball Club Mykolaiv (in Ukrainian: Муніципальний Баскетбольний Клуб Миколаїв), commonly known as simply Mykolaiv, is a professional basketball team (club) of Mykolaiv, Ukraine. They currently play in the Ukrainian SL Favorit Sport. ==History== In 2009, the team finished in fifth place in the Ukrainian Men's Basketball SuperLeague. In the 2009 NBA draft basketball player Sergiy Gladyr was the 49th draft pick, in the second round by the Atlanta Hawks, but did not succeed in the teams' request.
