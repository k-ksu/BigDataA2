M. Nazim Uddin Al Azad Bangladeshi Politician, journalist and former minister who was the Member of Parliament for the then Jessore-10 and Jessore-4 constituencies. ==Career== Azad was elected to parliament from Jessore-10 as an Bangladesh Nationalist Party candidate in 1979. He was elected to parliament from Jessore-4 as an Independent candidate in 1988.
