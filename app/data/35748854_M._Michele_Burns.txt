Martha Michele Burns (born February 1958 in Georgia) is an American businesswoman.NNDB She was chairman and CEO of Mercer between 2006 and 2011 and was director on the boards of a number of major American companies, including Cisco Systems, Wal-Mart, and Goldman Sachs. ==Education== She received a Bachelor of Business Administration summa cum laude from the University of Georgia in 1979, a Master of Accountancy from the same university in 1980.Kevin Roose, Goldman Adds a Board Member, The New York Times, October 28, 2011World Economic Forum Goldman Sachs Names Ex-Mercer CEO Michele Burns to Board, BusinessWeek, October 28, 2011 ==Early career== From 1981 to 1999, she worked for Arthur Andersen as a Senior Tax Partner.Goldman Sachs Board of Directors She served as Vice President for Corporate Tax of Delta Air Lines from 1999 to 2000, and as its Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer from 2000 to 2004.Forbes profile From 2004 to 2006, she served as EVP, CFO and restructuring officer of Mirant. ==CEO and director== From 2006 to 2011, she served as Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of Marsh & McLennan, and Chairman and CEO of Mercer.Wal-Mart Board of Directors She sits on the Boards of Directors of Cisco Systems.Retirement of John H.
