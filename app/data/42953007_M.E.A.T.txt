M.E.A.T. is a single released by the US musical group Tomahawk. It is a collection of two previously unreleased tracks from the Oddfellows recording session. It was first released on vinyl format on May 23, 2014 and was released digitally on June 4, 2014.Tomahawk To Release 'M.E.A.T.'
