M7GpppX diphosphatase (, DcpS, m7GpppX pyrophosphatase, m7GpppN m7GMP phosphohydrolase) is an enzyme with systematic name m7G5'ppp5'N m7GMP phosphohydrolase. This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction : (1) m7G5'ppp5'N(3'ppp5'N)n \+ H2O \rightleftharpoons 7-methylguanosine 5'-phosphate + pp5'N(3'ppp5'N)n : (2) 7-methylguanosine 5'-diphosphate + H2O \rightleftharpoons 7-methylguanosine 5'-phosphate + phosphate Decapping is a process in the control of eukaryotic mRNA degradation. == References == == External links == * Category:EC 3.6.1
