Sir Mutha Venkatasubba Rao (18 July 1878 – 30 December 1960) was an Indian lawyer, philanthropist, socialite and administrator. He served as a judge of the Madras High Court and Agent for Berar. == Early life and education == Venkatasubba Rao was born in Akividu Village in West Godavari District of Andhra Pradesh on 18 July 1878. He graduated from the Madras Christian College and studied law at the Madras Law College. == Career == On completion of his education, Venkatasubba Rao served as junior to C. V. Kumaraswami Sastri.
