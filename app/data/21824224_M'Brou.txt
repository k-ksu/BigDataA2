M'Brou is a village in southern Ivory Coast. It is in the sub-prefecture of Grand-Morié, Agboville Department, Agnéby-Tiassa Region, Lagunes District. M'Brou was a commune until March 2012, when it became one of 1126 communes nationwide that were abolished."Le gouvernement ivoirien supprime 1126 communes, et maintient 197 pour renforcer sa politique de décentralisation en cours", news.abidjan.net, 7 March 2012. ==Notes== Category:Former communes of Ivory Coast Category:Populated places in Lagunes District Category:Populated places in Agnéby-Tiassa
