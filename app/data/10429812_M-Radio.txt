M-Radio () is a Russian Moscow-based radio station. Their logo is a blue capital M with small, white, feathered wings extending from its sides. It started broadcasting in 1991 as «M-Radio. New Wave» in Moscow on 71.3 FM. At the moment it broadcasts to Moscow only on 96.4 FM and via Internet. They play a variety of rock music including Kaiser Chiefs, The Killers, Snow Patrol, U2, and many others. On October 1, 2015, the radio station began broadcasting in a web radio format.
