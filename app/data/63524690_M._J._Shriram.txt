M. J. Shriram is an Indian singer and actor who has worked in Tamil films. He is best known for his songs in Pachchak Kuthira (2006) and his role in Sivakasi (2005). == Career == Shiram began singing when he was five-years-old. His talent enabled him to share the stage with Kamal Haasan at a concert in Singapore in 1993. He played a small role in Sollamale (1998) before playing Vijay's friend in the film, Sivakasi (2005), which is considered his acting debut.
