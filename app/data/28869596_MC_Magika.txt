MC Magika (born Birmingham), is a British MC, music producer and rave promoter.Back To The Oldskool, MC Magika Interview He was regularly featured at many of the big UK raves in the mid to late 1990s including Dreamscape, Helter Skelter, Fantazia,MC Magika - Fantazia Exclusive Interview 2005 and on the dance music compilation Dancemania Speed sub-series.
