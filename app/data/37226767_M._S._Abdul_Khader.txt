M. S. Abdul Khader (c. 1932 - 3 October 2009) was an Indian politician of the Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK) and later, Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam. He served as mayor of Chennai and Member of Parliament. == Political career == Khader was elected mayor in December 1959, when the DMK won a majority of seats in the Corporation of Chennai.
