In September 2020, Niack signed to Warner Music. Niack's biggest hit "Oh Juliana" reached 247 million views on YouTube. == Career == In May 2020, Niack and Markin WF reached the top of the Spotify Brasil chart with the track "Na Raba Toma Tapão", in September the singer scored the hit "Oh Juliana" at the top of the chart Spotify Brasil, the song also entered the Spotify Global chart. Oh Juliana reached 62nd position on the Billboard Global Excl chart. U.S. charts and appeared on the Billboard Global 200 chart. Also in September, Niack signed on to Warner Music.
