M Kamalathal, affectionately known as the Idli Amma (mother) and Paatima (grandmother), is an Indian social worker known particularly for her work in making & selling Idlis to migrant workers and needy for INR 1. ==Social works== Kamalathal, an octogenarian and a resident of Vadivelampalayam on the Coimbatore city outskirts, is known for her selfless service-cum-low margin business model. She sells around 600 Idlis every day for INR 1 a piece with sambar and chutney side dishes, all of which she prepares alone. ==References== Category:Indian social workers Category:Living people Category:People from Coimbatore district Category:Year of birth missing (living people)
