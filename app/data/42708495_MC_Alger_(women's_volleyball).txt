Mouloudia Club d'Alger (), referred to as MC Alger or MCA for short, is an Algerian women's volleyball team that was founded on 1947, as a division of the of MC Alger. They play their home games in Hacène Harcha Arena, which has a capacity of 8,000 people. They were allocated one of two wildcard spots for the 2014 edition of FIVB Volleyball Women's Club World Championship held in Zurich, Switzerland after winning the African Championships.
