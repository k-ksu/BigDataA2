Maynard Freeman Schurman (born July 18, 1957) is a Canadian former professional ice hockey player. He played left wing in seven National Hockey League (NHL) games with the Hartford Whalers during the 1979–80 season. Schurman was born in Summerside, Prince Edward Island.
