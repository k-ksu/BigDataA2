M.T. Padma (born 9 January 1943, Kannur) is a politician from the Indian state of Kerala. ==Life== M.T. Padma was active in student politics, and served as vice president of the Kerala Students Union. She contested the 1982 Kerala Vidhan Sabha election as a Congress (I) candidate, but was not elected. She again contested the 1987 and 1991 elections, and was elected to represent the Koyilandy constituency.
