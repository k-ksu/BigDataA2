Radio m2o (stylized "radiom2o") is Italy's main 24-hour electronic music radio station, owned by the Gruppo Editoriale L'Espresso, ultimately owned by Exor (the Agnelli-family). It was founded in 2002. The station plays dance, EDM, trance, techno, house and sometimes urban music, among various others.
