M. S. Krishnan, or MSK, was an Indian trade unionist and leader of Communist Party of India. MSK became involved in student movement, and would leave his studies unfinished to become a full-time labour organizer in Bangalore. He was jailed on a number of occasions. He donated all property he had inherited to the Communist Party. MSK was elected to the Karnataka Legislative Assembly four times as a CPI candidate (1967, 1972, 1978, 1983) \- twice from the Malleswaram constituency and twice from the Rajajinagar constituency.Times of India.
