The M13 is a road in the Luapula Province of Zambia that connects Chipili with Kawambwa. It starts at a junction with the M3 Road (Kasama-Mansa Road) approximately 23 kilometres north of the Chipili town centre and it heads north for 88 kilometres to end in the town centre of Kawambwa at a junction with the D19, which goes to the town of Mporokoso in the east and the towns of Mbereshi and Nchelenge in the west. == See also == *Roads in Zambia == References == Category:Roads in Zambia Category:Luapula Province
